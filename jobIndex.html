About JobsWorld.com

JobWorld.com is one of the biggest job search networks for college students and recent graduates.

The site is exclusively targeted towards entry level hiring .Maintaining the largest database of colleges and students across various courses in India, it brings relevant employers and prospective students in touch with each other.

For fresh graduates, the site offers help to build the perfect CV, tips on personal grooming, and advice on how to present oneself to the perspective employer, thus making the job search simple and easier.

With an in-depth understanding of the entry level job market and a proven and successful background of the recruitment industry (being a naukri.com group portal), the site is well-equipped to market you in the best possible manner to the potential employers.

For the recruiters, the site is the perfect platform to reach an expansive database of talented, entry level employees. There is a wide range of recruitment solutions such as showcasing your company to the graduate community through online videos, presentations etc. This ensures that you are able to reach deserving candidates even in remote corners of the country. It's our endeavor to make your entry level hiring process easier and quicker.

The Company

Info Edge (India) Limited was incorporated on May 1, 1995 under the Companies Act, 1956. This was later converted into Public Limited on April 27, 2006.

Info Edge (India) Limited is a leading provider of online solutions in fields like recruitment, matrimonial & real estate classifieds and related services in India.

Major divisions of the company in online space are, the online recruitment division, the flagship brand www.naukri.com; online matrimonial division www.jeevansathi.com; real estate division www.99acres.com; www. naukrigulf.com for the Middle East online recruitment market; a career counseling and guidance site (www.asknaukri.com); a professional networking site (www.brijj.com); Shiksha.com to cater to the needs of education. The offline business includes an executive search firm called Quadrangle and allcheckdeals.com.

Winner of awards like "Red Herring Asia 100" 2006 for the INSTA HIRE Solution and "Silver Effies" 2006 Ideas that Work for "Hari Sadu" campaign, Info Edge (India) Limited has a collaboration with leading newspapers and magazines in India, along with a wide range of national and multi-national companies that access our databases for finding the right talent.

Info Edge (India) Limited currently has a network of 67 offices located in 41 cities throughout India, as well as two offices in Dubai and one in Bahrain, with an employee strength of 1,650 persons.
